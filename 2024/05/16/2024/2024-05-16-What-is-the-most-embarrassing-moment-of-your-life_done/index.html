1. English

What is the most embarrassing moment of your life?

Naveen Kumar Reddy · Worked at Ocwen

​ 579.7K views · 31.8k Upvotes

I work in an IT firm and it is pretty common for us to face issues with laptops. We have a service desk that solves our issues when a ticket is raised. One day I was facing some issue with Outlook and I raised a ticket to get it resolved. I got a call from Service desk lady after some time and it went this way:

Lady: Hi I’m calling from service desk, what is the problem?

Me: (I explained the problem).

Lady: Can you please share your screen and give me the control so that I can solve the issue?

Me: (I shared the screen and gave the control. She solved it!!)

Lady: You may close the ticket from your end, the issue is solved.

Me: Thank you very much, I will close the ticket.


I opened her profile in lync (it is business version of skype, where you can chat and view the profiles of your company mates etc.) and had a look at her profile. The display pic looked small in size. I instantly messaged her Employee ID to my colleague(my college mate) and asked him to have a look at her. He replied “Man!! She looks good in some angles.”

I tried searching her FB profile with the help of her name, but there were too many results.

I started searching on linked in instead, I found her profile this time. I was trying to enlarge the profile pic and suddenly a message popped up - it says

“You may please stop sharing your screen!!” and it is from the service desk lady. I didn’t know what to say. I thought WTF have I done, I logged out immediately.

Even though I still face problems with Outlook, I didn’t dare to raise any tickets out of embarrassment.

I work in an IT firm and it is pretty common for us to face issues with laptops. We have a service desk that solves our issues when a ticket is raised.
我在一家IT公司工作，我们经常遇到笔记本电脑的问题。我们有一个服务台，当提交工单时，他们会解决我们的问题。

One day I was facing some issue with Outlook and I raised a ticket to get it resolved.
有一天，我的Outlook出现了一些问题，于是我提交了一个工单来解决它。

I got a call from Service desk lady after some time and it went this way:
过了一段时间，我接到了服务台女士的电话，通话内容如下：

Lady: Hi I’m calling from service desk, what is the problem?
女士：你好，我是从服务台打来的，请问有什么问题？

Me: (I explained the problem).
我：（解释了问题）。

Lady: Can you please share your screen and give me the control so that I can solve the issue?
女士：你能分享你的屏幕并给我控制权吗，这样我就可以解决这个问题？

Me: (I shared the screen and gave the control. She solved it!!)
我：（分享了屏幕并给予控制权，她解决了！）

Lady: You may close the ticket from your end, the issue is solved.
女士：您可以关闭您的工单，问题已经解决了。

Me: Thank you very much, I will close the ticket.
我：谢谢你，我会关闭这张工单的。


I opened her profile in lync (it is business version of skype, where you can chat and view the profiles of your company mates etc.) and had a look at her profile. The display pic looked small in size.
我在Lync上打开了她的个人资料（这是Skype的商业版本，可以与公司同事聊天并查看他们的个人资料等），看了一下她的个人资料，显示图片看起来很小。

I instantly messaged her Employee ID to my colleague(my college mate) and asked him to have a look at her. He replied “Man!! She looks good in some angles.”
我立刻把她的员工ID发给我的同事（我的大学同学），让他看看。他回复说：“哥们儿！从某些角度来说，她确实很好看。

I tried searching her FB profile with the help of her name, but there were too many results.
我试图用她的名字搜索她在FB上的个人资料，但结果太多了。

I started searching on linked in instead, I found her profile this time. I was trying to enlarge the profile pic and suddenly a message popped up - it says
于是，我开始在LinkedIn上搜索，这次找到了她的档案。我正试图放大头像，突然弹出一条消息 —— 上面写着：

“You may please stop sharing your screen!!” and it is from the service desk lady. I didn’t know what to say. I thought WTF have I done, I logged out immediately.
“请停止共享你的屏幕！”这来自服务台的那位女士。我不知道该说什么，只能想自己到底干了什么，然后立即退出了登录。

Even though I still face problems with Outlook, I didn’t dare to raise any tickets out of embarrassment.
尽管我仍然还有Outlook的问题，但由于尴尬，我不敢再提任何请求单了。
