Why do people say “let love find you, don’t look for it”?

Rik Osborne · 2018 Top Writer, apparently. Journeyman cook, not a chef.

​ 673.2K views · 46.0k Upvotes

“If you sit home playing video games all day, you won’t meet anyone.”

I was 49 years old, and had never been married. In fact, I had barely dated as an adult. I’d had one relationship, when I was 23–24 years old, that lasted 18 months, and another when I was 36 that lasted six months. And after that, I just never had much interest in trying to find romance and love. I took the position, “if it happens, it happens”.

So, living alone in an apartment, at 49 years old, I spent most of my time alone, playing World of Warcraft, when I wasn’t at work. Work and WoW, that was my life.


So on Christmas Eve, 2015, I was sitting alone in my apartment, playing WoW. At some point in the evening, I wandered downstairs to my kitchen, and as I was entering the kitchen, I noticed for the first time a small, patched section on the wall. I was curious about it. You see, my apartment building was built some time before World War 2. I had already figured out that, when the building was new, it was heated by some kind of furnace in the basement, with all of the apartments sharing the heat. I figured that this small, square, patched-over spot must have originally been some kind of vent that allowed the air to move between apartments. My curiosity prompted me to knock on it.

Somebody knocked back.

So I knocked again, and heard more knocks in return. Then I heard a woman’s voice through the wall yelling, “Hey, come over here!”

I was … a bit surprised. But I shrugged and went over there.

I met my neighbor for the first time. We spent the rest of Christmas Eve together. Then we spent Christmas together. And the next day. And the next, and the next. She had a big TV, but no cable, so she could only use it to watch DVDs. I set her TV up with my WiFi password so that she could use my Netflix account. I spent most evenings in her apartment, watching shows on Netflix with her.


In March 2016, she moved into my apartment, and toward the end of April, we got married.

I was playing video games instead of looking for love, but love found me :)

为什么人们常说“不要试图去找寻爱情，而是让它来找你”？_Quora文选

“If you sit home playing video games all day, you won’t meet anyone.”
“如果你整天待在家里玩电子游戏，你是不会遇到任何人的。”

I was 49 years old, and had never been married. In fact, I had barely dated as an adult. I’d had one relationship, when I was 23–24 years old, that lasted 18 months, and another when I was 36 that lasted six months. And after that, I just never had much interest in trying to find romance and love. I took the position, “if it happens, it happens”.
我已经49岁了，从未结过婚。事实上，成年后我几乎没有约会过。我有过一段恋情，在我23到24岁时，持续了18个月；还有一段是在我36岁时，维持了六个月。而之后，我对寻找浪漫和爱情就不再抱有太大兴趣了。我采取的态度是，“随缘吧”。

So, living alone in an apartment, at 49 years old, I spent most of my time alone, playing World of Warcraft, when I wasn’t at work. Work and WoW; that was my life.
于是，在49岁的年纪，我独自住在公寓里，大部分时间都一个人度过，不上班的时候就在玩《魔兽世界》。工作和《魔兽世界》，这就是我的生活。


So on Christmas Eve, 2015, I was sitting alone in my apartment, playing WoW.
于是，在2015年的平安夜，我独自一人坐在公寓里玩《魔兽世界》。

At some point in the evening, I wandered downstairs to my kitchen, and as I was entering the kitchen, I noticed for the first time a small, patched section on the wall.
那天晚上的某个时刻，我漫步到楼下的厨房，当我进入厨房时，第一次注意到墙上有一个小小的修补过的地方。

I was curious about it. You see, my apartment building was built some time before World War 2.
我对此感到好奇，要知道，我住的这栋公寓是在二战前建的。

I had already figured out that when the building was new, it was heated by some kind of furnace in the basement with all of the apartments sharing the heat.
我已经了解到这栋建筑刚建成的时候，是通过地下室的一种炉子供暖，所有公寓共享热量。

I figured that this small square patched-over spot must have originally been some kind of vent that allowed air to move between apartments.
我猜想这个被修补的小方块原本应该是一个通风口，用来让空气在各个公寓之间流通。

My curiosity prompted me to knock on it.
我的好奇心驱使我敲了敲它。

Somebody knocked back.
有人回应了我的敲击声。

So I knocked again and heard more knocks in return. Then I heard a woman’s voice through the wall yelling,
“Hey come over here!”
于是我又敲了一次，并听到了更多回响声，然后透过墙壁传来了一个女人喊叫的声音：“嘿，过来呀！”

I was … a bit surprised. But I shrugged and went over there.
我……有点惊讶。但还是耸耸肩走了过去。

I met my neighbor for the first time. We spent rest of Christmas Eve together. Then we spent Christmas together. And next day.And next,and next.She had big TV but no cable so she could only use it watch DVDs.I set her TV up with WiFi password so she could use Netflix account.I spent most evenings her apartment watching shows Netflix her
这是我们第一次见面，我们一起度过了剩下的平安夜，然后一起庆祝圣诞节，还有接下来的一天，又一天。她有一台大电视，但没有有线电视，所以只能用来看DVD。我帮她连上了我的WiFi密码，让她可以使用我的Netflix账号。从此，大部分晚上我们都在她家看Netflix上的节目。


In March 2016, she moved into my apartment, and toward the end of April, we got married.
2016年3月，她搬进了我的公寓，到了4月底，我们结婚了。

I was playing video games instead of looking for love, but love found me :)
我本来是在玩电子游戏，而不是在寻找爱情，但爱情找到了我 :)
