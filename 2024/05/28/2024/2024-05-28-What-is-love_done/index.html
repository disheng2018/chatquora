What is love?

Grace Ng · Co-Founder, Javelin.com

​ 346.9K views · 6.5k Upvotes

I just found this, it’s pretty touching and interesting to see how kids think about this subject.

A group of professional people posed this question to a group of 4 to 8 year-olds, ‘What does love mean?’
The answers they got were broader and deeper than anyone could have imagined


  • ‘When my grandmother got arthritis, she couldn’t bend over and paint her toenails anymore. So my grandfather does it for her all the time, even when his hands got arthritis too. That’s love.’ —— Rebecca- age 8

  • ‘Love is when you go out to eat and give somebody most of your French fries without making them give you any of theirs.’ —— Chrissy - age 6

  • ‘Love is what makes you smile when you’re tired.’ —— Terri - age 4

  • ‘Love is when my mommy makes coffee for my daddy and she takes a sip before giving it to him, to make sure the taste is OK.’ —— Danny - age 7

  • ‘Love is when you kiss all the time.. Then when you get tired of kissing, you still want to be together and you talk more. My Mommy and Daddy are like that. They look gross when they kiss.’ —— Emily - age 8

  • ‘Love is what’s in the room with you at Christmas if you stop opening presents and listen.’ —— Bobby - age 7 (Wow!)

  • ‘If you want to learn to love better, you should start with a friend who you hate.’ —— Nikka - age 6 (we need a few million more Nikka’s on this planet)

  • ‘Love is when you tell a guy you like his shirt, then he wears it everyday.’—— Noelle - age 7

  • ‘Love is like a little old woman and a little old man who are still friends even after they know each other so well.’ —— Tommy - age 6

  • ‘During my piano recital, I was on a stage and I was scared. I looked at all the people watching me and saw my daddy waving and smiling. He was the only one doing that. I wasn’t scared anymore..’ —— Cindy - age 8

  • ‘My mommy loves me more than anybody. You don’t see anyone else kissing me to sleep at night.’ —— Clare - age 6

  • ‘Love is when Mommy sees Daddy smelly and sweaty and still says he is handsomer than Robert Redford.’ —— Chris - age 7

  • ‘Love is when your puppy licks your face even after you left him alone all day.’ —— Mary Ann - age 4

  • ‘When you love somebody, your eyelashes go up and down and little stars come out of you.’ —— Karen - age 7

2. 中英译文

What is love?
什么是爱？

I just found this, it’s pretty touching and interesting to see how kids think about this subject.
我刚发现这个故事，看到孩子们对这个话题的想法，真是既感动又有趣。

A group of professional people posed this question to a group of 4 to 8 year-olds, ‘What does love mean?’
一群专业人士向一群4到8岁的孩子们提出了这样一个问题，“什么是爱？”

The answers they got were broader and deeper than anyone could have imagined ——
他们得到的回答比任何人想象的都要广泛和深刻：


  • ‘When my grandmother got arthritis, she couldn’t bend over and paint her toenails anymore. So my grandfather does it for her all the time, even when his hands got arthritis too. That’s love…’. Rebecca - age 8
    “当我奶奶得了关节炎，不能弯腰涂脚趾甲时，我爷爷就一直帮她涂，即便他自己后来也患上了关节炎。这就是爱。”——Rebecca，8岁

  • ‘When someone loves you, The way they say your name is different. You just know that your name is safe in their mouth.’. Billy - age 4
    “当有人爱你时，他们说你名字的方式就会不一样。你会知道你的名字从他们嘴里说出来是安全的。”——Billy，4岁

  • ‘Love is when a girl puts on perfume and a boy puts on shaving cologne and they go out and smell each other.’ Karl - age 5
    “爱是当一个女孩喷上香水，一个男孩用上剃须水，他们外出，这就是所谓的女为悦己者容。”——Karl，5岁

  • ‘Love is when you go out to eat and give somebody most of your French fries without making them give you any of theirs.’ Chrissy - age 6
    “爱是当你外出吃饭时，给别人你大部分的薯条而不要求他们给你任何薯条。”——Chrissy，6岁

  • ‘Love is what makes you smile when you’re tired.’ Terri - age 4
    “爱是当你疲倦时，仍能让你微笑的东西。”——Terri，4岁

  • ‘Love is when my mommy makes coffee for my daddy and she takes a sip before giving it to him, to make sure the taste is OK.’ Danny - age 7
    “爱是当我妈妈给我爸爸冲咖啡时，她会先喝一口，确保咖啡的味道是好的。”——Danny，7岁

  • ‘Love is when you kiss all the time.. Then when you get tired of kissing, you still want to be together and you talk more. My Mommy and Daddy are like that.. They look gross when they kiss.’ Emily - age 8
    “爱是当你们一直亲吻，然后即使厌倦了亲吻，你们仍然想在一起并多聊天。我爸妈就是这样的，他们接吻时看起来很恶心。”——Emily，8岁

  • ‘Love is what’s in the room with you at Christmas if you stop opening presents and listen.’ Bobby - age 7 (Wow!)
    “爱是当你在圣诞节停下来不再拆礼物，静静聆听时，和你在同一房间里的那种感觉。”——Bobby，7岁 (哇！)

  • ‘If you want to learn to love better, you should start with a friend who you hate.’ Nikka - age 6 (we need a few million more Nikka’s on this planet)
    “如果你想学会更好地去爱，你应该从你讨厌的朋友开始。”——Nikka，6岁（我们需要这个世界上再多几百万个Nikka）

  • ‘Love is when you tell a guy you like his shirt, then he wears it everyday.’ Noelle - age 7
    “爱是当你告诉一个男孩你喜欢他的衬衫，然后他每天都穿那件衬衫。”——Noelle，7岁

  • ‘Love is like a little old woman and a little old man who are still friends even after they know each other so well.’ Tommy - age 6
    “爱就像一位老太太和一位老先生，即使他们彼此非常了解，仍然是朋友。”——Tommy，6岁

  • ‘During my piano recital, I was on a stage and I was scared. I looked at all the people watching me and saw my daddy waving and smiling. He was the only one doing that. I wasn’t scared anymore.’ Cindy - age 8
    “在我的钢琴演奏会上，我站在舞台上，非常害怕。我看着台下所有的人，看到我爸爸挥手微笑。他是唯一一个这样做的人，这让我不再害怕了。”——Cindy，8岁

  • ‘My mommy loves me more than anybody. You don’t see anyone else kissing me to sleep at night.’ Clare - age 6
    “我妈妈比任何人都爱我，你不会看到其他人在晚上吻我入睡。”——Clare，6岁

  • ‘Love is when Mommy sees Daddy smelly and sweaty and still says he is handsomer than Robert Redford.’ Chris - age 7
    “爱是当妈妈看到爸爸汗水淋漓、气味难闻，仍然说他比Robert Redford更帅。”——Chris，7岁

  • ‘Love is when your puppy licks your face even after you left him alone all day.’ Mary Ann - age 4
    “爱是当你整天把小狗单独留在家里，当你回来时它仍然舔你的脸。”——Mary Ann，4岁

  • ‘I know my older sister loves me because she gives me all her old clothes and has to go out and buy new ones.’ Lauren - age 4
    “我知道我姐姐爱我，因为她把所有旧衣服都给了我，还得去买新衣服。”——Lauren，4岁

  • ‘When you love somebody, your eyelashes go up and down and little stars come out of you.’ Karen - age 7
    “当你爱一个人时，你的睫毛会上下跳动，并且会有小星星从你身上冒出来。”——Karen，7岁

  • ‘Love is when Mommy sees Daddy on the toilet and she doesn’t think it’s gross.’ Mark - age 6
    “爱是当妈妈看到爸爸在上厕所，她不觉得恶心。”——Mark，6岁

  • ‘You really shouldn’t say ‘I love you’ unless you mean it.. But if you mean it, you should say it a lot. People forget.’ Jessica - age 8
    “你真的不应该说'我爱你’，除非你是真的这么想。但如果你是真的这么想的，你应该多说，人们会忘记这个事儿。”——Jessica，8岁

And the final one
最后一个

The winner was a four-year-old child whose next-door neighbor was an elderly gentleman who had recently lost his wife. Upon seeing the man cry, the little boy went into the old gentleman’s yard, climbed onto his lap, and just sat there. When his Mother asked what he had said to the neighbor, the little boy said, ‘Nothing, I just helped him cry.’
获胜者是一个四岁的孩子，他的隔壁邻居是一位刚失去妻子的老先生。看到老人哭泣，这个小男孩走进老先生的院子，爬到他的腿上，安静地坐在那里。当他妈妈问他对邻居说了什么时，小男孩回答说，“什么也没说，我只是陪他一起哭。”
