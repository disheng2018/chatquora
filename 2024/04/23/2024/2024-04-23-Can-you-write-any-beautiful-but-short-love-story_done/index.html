1. English

Can you write any beautiful, but short, love story?

Roma Dilip Shahni · Romancing life!

​ 428.5K views · 18.6k Upvotes

‘Send nudes!’ the text read.

‘I’m not comfortable’, she replied.

‘So you don’t trust me?’ asked the love of her life.

‘That is not the case. You know I’m not comfortable with sexing and pictures. You know I love you, I just need some time.’

‘You’ve taken your time. Send nudes or find someone with an equally non existent libido as yours.’, the ultimatum was received.

She shifted in her seat and closed her eyes, considered the deal and decided to reply. Switched on her front camera and took a shot.


Her boyfriend received the image and downloaded instantly. A naked middle finger he saw!

‘Fuck off!’ the caption read and within minutes he could no longer send her text messages.

That day she chose the most important love of all: self love!

She fell for herself and decided she deserved nothing but love. Ever witnessed a more beautiful love story?


Edit:

To everyone asking if this happened with me or is a work of fiction, I don’t think that matters. This writing is my message to every girl who’s contemplating taking off her dress only in an attempt to keep him. Please realize what is love and how much effort is desirable to keep a relationship alive. Please be safe!

“我不舒服”，她回答道。

‘So you don’t trust me?’ asked the love of her life.
“那么你就是不信任我？”她这一生的爱人问道。

‘That is not the case. You know I’m not comfortable with sexing and pictures. You know I love you, I just need some time.’
“情况并非如此。你知道我对性感照片和图片感到不自在。你知道我爱你，我只是需要一些时间。”

‘You’ve taken your time. Send nudes or find someone with an equally non existent libido as yours.’, the ultimatum was received.
“你已经花了足够的时间了，发果照或者找一个和你一样根本没有xing欲的人”，最后通牒已发出。

She shifted in her seat and closed her eyes, considered the deal and decided to reply. Switched on her front camera and took a shot.
她在座位上挪动，闭上眼睛，考虑这个提议并决定回复，打开前置摄像头拍了一张照片。

Her boyfriend received the image and downloaded instantly. A naked middle finger he saw!
她的男友接收到这张图片并立即下载下来，他看见的是一个赤裸的中指！

‘Fuck off!’ the caption read and within minutes he could no longer send her text messages.
标题写着：“滚开！”几分钟内他再也无法给她发送信息了。

That day she chose the most important love of all: self love!
那天，她选择了所有爱中最重要的：自爱！

She fell for herself and decided she deserved nothing but love. Ever witnessed a more beautiful love story?
她迷恋上自己，并决定除了爱之外什么都不配拥有。还有比这更美丽的爱情故事吗？
