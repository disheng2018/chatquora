1. English

What 5 things can I do now to have a better life and future?

Abinash Mishra · IAS | IIT Kharagpur

​ 129.5K views · 5.6k Upvotes

  1. Do not use your smart phone 2–3 hours before you sleep and 2–3 hours after waking up. Check only if something is urgent. Give time to yourself
  2. Meet people from all area: scientists, farmers, administrators, politicians. Talk to them with an open heart. Your outlook will get broadened.
  3. Circle: Have a small, but good friend circle. Friends, we become like our friends with time. So choose carefully. Your association will decide how far you will go.
  4. Try to become self dependent in everything. If you want knowledge, start learning on your own. If you want money, start earning on your own. It makes you capable, free and confident.
  5. Write a diary and carry a paper always to note down what you are doing right now. Just do this for few days. See the change with in yourself.

2. 中英译文

为了拥有更好的生活我们现在应该做哪5件事？_Quora文选

  1. Do not use your smart phone 2–3 hours before you sleep and 2–3 hours after waking up. Check only if something is urgent. Give time to yourself
    请在睡前2-3小时及醒后的2-3小时内不要使用智能手机，仅在紧急情况下检查手机。给自己一些时间。

  2. Meet people from all area: scientists, farmers, administrators, politicians. Talk to them with an open heart. Your outlook will get broadened.
    结识来自各行各业的人：科学家、农民、行政管理者、政治家。以开放的心态与他们交谈，你的视野将因此而拓宽。

  3. Circle: Have a small, but good friend circle. Friends, we become like our friends with time. So choose carefully. Your association will decide how far you will go.
    圈子：建立一个小而精的朋友圈。朋友，随着时间我们会变得越来越像我们的朋友，所以要慎重选择，你所处的社交圈将决定你能走多远。

  4. Try to become self dependent in everything. If you want knowledge, start learning on your own. If you want money, start earning on your own. *It makes you capable, free and confident.
    尝试在一切事物上成为独立自主之人，如果你渴求知识，那就开始自学；如果你想要金钱，那就开始自食其力。这让你变得有能力、自由且充满信心。

  5. Write a diary and carry a paper always to note down what you are doing right now. Just do this for few days See the change within yourself.
    始终携带纸笔，记录下当前正在做什么事情，并坚持写日记，只需做几天，便可见证内心深处发生的变化。
