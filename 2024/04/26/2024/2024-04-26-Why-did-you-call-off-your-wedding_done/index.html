Why did you call off your wedding?

Anonymous · 6y

​ 122K views · 5.6k Upvotes

Called it off two days before wedding.

It was an arranged marriage. Got engaged to him after a month from meeting him. He insisted we get to know each other before tying the knot so we used to date for a while.

He wasn’t my type at all but I was under enormous pressure to get married and got tired of seeing boys. I really went into a state of confusion a month before the wedding.

He was controlling,manipulative and abusive. He threatened to commit suicide as it’s a prestige issue for him if marriage gets canceled and also blackmailed that he’ll leak our private messages and pictures. I got scared. Even my parents were not supportive about calling it off as they liked him.

Finally I decided to say no two days before the wedding as I was very depressed and wasn’t feeling one bit happy about the marriage. It was difficult but I did it.

A month after canceling the wedding, we came to know the boy had lied about his age,education and salary in the matrimonial profile and was already married. I was really outraged at my parents for not doing a proper background check on the boy.

My gut instincts said “no” from the day I met him. I wish I trusted myself and never gave up for my parents or forced myself into a relationship with him. But anyways it was one of the best decision I made in my life. Not getting married to some lying, abusive, blackmailing lowlife.

你为什么取消了你的婚礼？_Quora文选

Called it off two days before wedding.
在婚礼前两天取消了。

It was an arranged marriage. Got engaged to him after a month from meeting him. He insisted we get to know each other before tying the knot so we used to date for a while.
这是一桩包办婚姻，在遇见他一个月后我与他订婚了。他坚持我们在结婚前要相互了解，所以我们约会了一段时间。

He wasn’t my type at all but I was under enormous pressure to get married and got tired of seeing boys. I really went into a state of confusion a month before the wedding.
他根本不是我的类型，但我当时面临着巨大的压力要结婚，并且厌倦了见不同的男人。婚礼前一个月我陷入了困惑。

He was controlling, manipulative and abusive. He threatened to commit suicide as it’s a prestige issue for him if marriage gets canceled and also blackmailed that he’ll leak our private messages and pictures. I got scared. Even my parents were not supportive about calling it off as they liked him.
他控制欲强、操纵他人，还有虐待行为。他威胁说如果婚礼取消，他将自杀，因为对他来说这是面子问题，并且还勒索说他会泄露我们的私人信息和照片。我害怕了。即使我的父母也不支持我取消婚礼，因为他们喜欢他。

Finally I decided to say no two days before the wedding as I was very depressed and wasn’t feeling one bit happy about the marriage. It was difficult but I did it.
最终，我在婚礼前两天决定说不，因为我非常沮丧，对这段婚姻一点也不开心。这很困难，但我做到了。

A month after canceling the wedding, we came to know the boy had lied about his age, education and salary in the matrimonial profile and was already married. I was really outraged at my parents for not doing a proper background check on the boy.
取消婚礼一个月后，我们发现这个男孩在婚介资料中谎报了他的年龄、教育和薪水，并且他已经结婚了，我对我父母没有对这个男孩进行适当的背景调查感到非常愤怒。

My gut instincts said “no” from the day I met him. I wish I trusted myself and never gave up for my parents or forced myself into a relationship with him. But anyways it was one of the best decision I made in my life. Not getting married to some lying, abusive, blackmailing lowlife.
从我遇见他的那天起，我的直觉就说“不”。我希望我相信自己，永远不要为了父母而放弃，也不要强迫自己与他建立关系。但无论如何，这是我一生中做出的最好的决定之一。没有嫁给一个撒谎、虐待、敲诈勒索的人渣。
