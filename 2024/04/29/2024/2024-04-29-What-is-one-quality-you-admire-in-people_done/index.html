1. English

What is one quality you admire in people?

Abinash Mishra ·

​ 177.2K views · 12.1k Upvotes

Yesterday I was in Kanpur for my friend’s wedding.

In the morning I woke up to people saying , “refused to take the car”.

We quickly caught up with everything that happened.

It was my friend, the groom who denied to take the car.


The First thing he felt was that it was against his self respect since he wants to buy a car with his

own money.

Secondly, the girl’s father died years ago and her brother and mother worked hard to educate the kids. Her brother ensured that his sister will have the best marriage function.

The girl’s family insisted as dowry still plays a part in social prestige in India.

But finally my friend was able to convince them and came home with the bride happily without the car.


Dowry is a social evil. We all know this. Yet how many stand against it. Even the most educated ones demand the most.

Yet this guy stood up to his values and politely refused.

This is called honesty.

2. 中英译文

你欣赏人的哪种品质？_Quora文选

Yesterday I was in Kanpur for my friend’s wedding.
昨日，我因朋友的婚礼而身处坎普尔。

In the morning I woke up to people saying , “refused to take the car”.
清晨醒来，耳边传来人们议论着，“拒绝接受那辆车”。

We quickly caught up with everything that happened.
我们迅速了解了所有发生的事情。

It was my friend, the groom who denied to take the car.
原来是我的朋友，新郎本人拒绝接收那辆嫁妆车。


The First thing he felt was that it was against his self respect since he wants to buy a car with his own money.
首先他觉得这违背了他的自尊，因为他想用自己的钱买车。

Secondly, the girl’s father died years ago and her brother and mother worked hard to educate the kids. Her brother ensured that his sister will have the best marriage function.
其次，女方的父亲多年前就去世了，她的哥哥和母亲努力工作以供孩子们接受教育。她的哥哥确保妹妹能有一个最好的婚礼仪式。

The girl’s family insisted as dowry still plays a part in social prestige in India.
女方家庭坚持要求提供嫁妆，因为在印度社会中声望仍然很重要。

But finally my friend was able to convince them and came home with the bride happily without the car.
但最终我的朋友成功说服了他们，并且没有那辆车也高高兴兴地带着新娘回家了。

Dowry is a social evil. We all know this. Yet how many stand against it. Even the most educated ones demand the most.
彼此皆知，《聘金》乃社会之恶。然而反对者几何？即便是学识渊博者也索求无度。

Yet this guy stood up to his values and politely refused.
然而此君坚守价值观，并以礼貌拒绝。

This is called honesty.
这便是所谓之诚信。
