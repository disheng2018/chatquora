What is the sweetest thing you have ever seen a husband do for a wife?

Anonymous · Updated 7y

​ 118.7K views · 5.2k Upvotes

Our’s is a love marriage. Initially, My husband’s parents didn’t appprove of our relationship . I lost my parents while I was young. Later,upon seeing him in depression they finally gave in. So, now we are happily married for three years.

Two years passed by.


Our parents asked us now and then about KIDS. My in-laws are conservative. As most of the Indian parents, they too feel that kids complete the family. They suggested us to consult a doctor. Everyone suggested indeed. At the very sight of the hospital, my husband stopped the car to a side. he held me by the hand and said:

“Do we really need children? Look.. My first ever kid is YOU. You complete me. I’m fine with one.”

he said in a dejected tone. ALL I COULD DO WAS SMILE. .. thanking the almighty for giving me the most heartful thing ever lived. But this did not end here.

We went through the checkup. The reports are out. The doctor revealed in a dejected tone that I CANNOT CONCEIVE.

Tears flooded my eyes. That moment, I collapsed to the ground in tears. I’m unable to see anything. Tears obscured my vision. All I remember was him carefully carrying me to the car in his hands. We went home. I went to our bedroom and locked the door. I cried. I cried. Later in the evening my mother-in-law came to our room. She didn’t say anything.

After fifteen minutes of complete silence she said, “I’m sorry beta. We never knew this thing about Akansh.” She broke down and left in tears. I was left in an utter dismay. Later upon asking him, he said “I will never cause any pain to you by any means.”


He lied to his parents that the problem lies within him.

“What did I do to deserve him?” He knows that I could not bear that guilt for longer. He immediately applied for a position in abroad in the same company on a two year contract and here we are, in LONDON away from home, away from everything.

He feels that time heals everything.

2. 中英译文

你所见过的丈夫为妻子做过的最甜蜜的事情是什么？_Quora文选

Our’s is a love marriage. Initially, My husband’s parents didn’t appprove of our relationship . I lost my parents while I was young. Later,upon seeing him in depression they finally gave in. So, now we are happily married for three years.
我们的婚姻是建立在爱情之上的。起初，我的丈夫的父母并不赞成我们的关系。我年轻时就失去了双亲。后来，看到他因此陷入抑郁，他们终于同意了。如今，我们已经幸福地结婚三年了。

Two years passed by.
两年过去了。

Our parents asked us now and then about KIDS. My in-laws are conservative. As most of the Indian parents, they too feel that kids complete the family. They suggested us to consult a doctor. Everyone suggested indeed. At the very sight of the hospital, my husband stopped the car to a side. he held me by the hand and said:
我们的父母偶尔会问起孩子的事情。我的公婆很保守，和大多数印度家长一样，他们也认为孩子使得家庭完整。他们建议我们去看医生，并且每个人都这么说着确实如此，在医院门口一见，我丈夫就将车停在了一边，握住我的手说：

“Do we really need children? Look.. My first ever kid is YOU. You complete me. I’m fine with one.”
"我们真的需要孩子吗？看... 我有过的第一个孩子就是你，你让我感到完整，对我来说只有你就足够了。”

he said in a dejected tone. ALL I COULD DO WAS SMILE. .. thanking the almighty for giving me the most heartful thing ever lived. But this did not end here.
他用一种沮丧的语气说道。我所能做的只是微笑...感谢那位全能者，赐予了我这个世上最珍贵的恩物。但事情并未就此结束。

We went through the checkup. The reports are out. The doctor revealed in a dejected tone that I CANNOT CONCEIVE.
我们完成了检查，报告出炉了。医生以沮丧的语气透露，我无法怀孕。

Tears flooded my eyes. That moment, I collapsed to the ground in tears. I’m unable to see anything. Tears obscured my vision. All I remember was him carefully carrying me to the car in his hands. We went home.
泪水模糊了我的双眼。那一刻，我泪流满面地倒在地上。什么也看不见，泪水遮蔽了我的视线。我唯一记得的是他小心翼翼地把我抱起来放进车里，我们回家了。

I went to our bedroom and locked the door. I cried. I cried.
我走进我们的卧室锁上门，哭泣着、不停地哭泣着。

Later in the evening my mother-in-law came to our room. She didn’t say anything.
傍晚时分，我的婆婆来到我们房间，并没有说什么话。

After fifteen minutes of complete silence she said, “I’m sorry beta.We never knew this thing about Akansh.” She broke down and left in tears.
经过十五分钟彻底的寂静之后她说：“对不起啊孩子们。” 我们从未知晓关于Akansh的这件事，她情绪崩溃离开时已是泪流满面。

I was left in an utter dismay. Later upon asking him, he said “I will never cause any pain to you by any means.”
完全茫然中留下了我。后来询问他时，他说：“无论如何，都不会让你承受任何痛苦。”

He lied to his parents that the problem lies within him
他向父母撒谎称问题出在自己身上

“What did i do deserve him?” He knows that i could not bear that guilt for longer.He immediately applied for a position abroad same company on two year contract and here we are,in LONDON away from home ,away from everything
“何其有幸拥有他（我上辈子做了什么好事儿才配得上他呢？）”，他知道长久以往我难以承受这份罪恶感。于是立即申请同公司海外职位，签订了两年合约；如今，我们在伦敦远离家乡、远离一切。

He feels time heals everything
他相信时间能治愈一切。
